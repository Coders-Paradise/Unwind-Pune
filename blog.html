This blog will teach you beginner to advanced Music Theory, and how to apply these techniques in composing and arranging for fingerstyle guitar. Some of the same principles can also be applied to other instruments.

It contains helpful resources like videos and website links to help you learn more about these topics. The entirety of music theory is far too complex to show on one website. The posts are given in order, based on difficulty. Each post covers a different topic.

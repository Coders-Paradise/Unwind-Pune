What does our campaign do?


  • Hold webinars with our therapists at schools and orphanages in your state/country to spread awareness on mental health, break the stigma by debunking myths and make people aware of the available resources and make them accessible
  • Advocate for the importance of the availability of a trained mental health therapist in schools. (Note: a guidance counselor and a mental health therapist are two different people)

Who’s our target audience?


  • High-school directors and principals
  • High-school students
  • Teachers
  • Parents

Departments:

  1. Research

    • Research-oriented team
    • Helping to compile research reports and draft ideas on what to hold the event around.
    • Will help research existing mental health resources specific to the city/country and also making UNWIND’s resources accessible

  2. Advocacy

    • Outreach-oriented team
    • Contacting local schools, orphanages, and other educational institutes for the webinar and advocacy for the importance of a therapist in the above-mentioned institutions

  3. Education

    • Teaching and tech-oriented team
    • Identifying target schools/orphanages within the region to host webinars.
    • Teaching Zoom workshops at schools using our curriculum and contact templates.
    • Tracking student engagement

  4. Other

    • Hosting local events (possibly in person post-COVID)
    • Fundraising
